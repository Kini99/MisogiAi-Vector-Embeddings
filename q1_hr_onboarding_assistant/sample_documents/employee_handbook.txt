EMPLOYEE HANDBOOK
TechCorp Solutions Inc.

SECTION 1: VACATION AND LEAVE POLICIES

Vacation Days
All full-time employees are entitled to 20 vacation days per year. New employees receive 15 vacation days in their first year of employment. Vacation days accrue monthly and can be carried over to the next year up to a maximum of 10 days.

Sick Leave
Employees are entitled to 10 sick days per year. Sick leave is separate from vacation time and cannot be carried over. A doctor's note may be required for absences longer than 3 consecutive days.

Parental Leave
New parents are eligible for 12 weeks of parental leave. This includes both maternity and paternity leave. Employees must provide 30 days advance notice when possible. Parental leave can be taken within 12 months of the child's birth or adoption.

Holidays
The company observes the following holidays: New Year's Day, Memorial Day, Independence Day, Labor Day, Thanksgiving Day, Christmas Day, and Martin Luther King Jr. Day.

SECTION 2: HEALTH AND BENEFITS

Health Insurance
Employees are eligible for health insurance coverage on the first day of the month following 30 days of employment. The company offers three health plan options: Basic, Standard, and Premium. The company covers 80% of the premium cost for all plans.

Dental Insurance
Dental coverage is available through our preferred provider network. The plan covers 100% of preventive care, 80% of basic procedures, and 50% of major procedures. Annual maximum benefit is $2,000 per person.

Vision Insurance
Vision coverage includes annual eye exams and allowances for frames and lenses. The plan covers up to $200 for frames and $150 for lenses every 24 months.

Retirement Benefits
The company offers a 401(k) plan with matching contributions. The company matches 50% of employee contributions up to 6% of salary. Employees are eligible to participate after 90 days of employment.

SECTION 3: WORK ARRANGEMENTS

Remote Work Policy
Employees may work remotely up to 3 days per week with manager approval. Remote work arrangements must be documented and reviewed quarterly. Employees working remotely must maintain regular communication with their team and attend all scheduled meetings.

Hybrid Work Schedule
The company supports hybrid work arrangements. Employees can choose to work from the office 2-3 days per week and remotely for the remaining days. Core hours for collaboration are 10:00 AM to 3:00 PM.

Office Hours
Standard office hours are 9:00 AM to 5:00 PM, Monday through Friday. Flexible scheduling is available with manager approval. Employees are expected to work 40 hours per week.

SECTION 4: CONDUCT AND ETHICS

Dress Code
Business casual attire is required in the office. This includes collared shirts, slacks, skirts, and appropriate footwear. Jeans are allowed on Fridays. Employees representing the company at external meetings should dress more formally.

Social Media Policy
Employees should be mindful of their social media presence and avoid posting content that could reflect negatively on the company. When posting about work-related topics, employees should include a disclaimer that views are their own.

Code of Conduct
All employees are expected to maintain professional behavior and treat colleagues with respect. Harassment, discrimination, and bullying will not be tolerated. Employees should report any concerns to HR immediately.

SECTION 5: COMPENSATION AND PERFORMANCE

Salary Reviews
Performance reviews are conducted annually in March. Salary increases are based on performance, market conditions, and company profitability. Employees can request a salary review outside the annual cycle for exceptional circumstances.

Bonus Structure
Annual bonuses are based on individual performance and company results. Target bonus is 10% of base salary for individual contributors and 15% for managers. Bonuses are paid in February of the following year.

Promotion Process
Promotions are based on performance, skills, and business needs. Employees interested in promotion should discuss career goals with their manager during regular one-on-one meetings.

SECTION 6: GENERAL POLICIES

Employee Portal
All employees have access to the employee portal where they can view pay stubs, update personal information, request time off, and access company resources. The portal is available 24/7 at employee.techcorp.com.

Onboarding Process
New employees complete a comprehensive onboarding program over their first 30 days. This includes orientation, training, and introductions to key team members. HR will schedule regular check-ins during the first 90 days.

Contact Information
For HR-related questions, employees can contact the HR department at hr@techcorp.com or call (555) 123-4567. The HR office is located on the 3rd floor and is open Monday through Friday, 9:00 AM to 5:00 PM.
